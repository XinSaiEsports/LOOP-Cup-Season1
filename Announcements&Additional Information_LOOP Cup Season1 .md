-   Open Registration Period: February 27, 2026, 00:00 to March 5, 2026, 24:00 (Beijing Time)

-   Closed Qualifier Dates: March 14-15, 2026 (Beijing Time)

-   Playoffs Dates: March 21–22, 2026 (Beijing Time)

-   Tournament Operator:XinSai Esports（XSE）。

-   Tournament Tier:Tier 2。

## Team Registration

-   This tournament does not include Direct VRS Invites based on VRS list; all participants will be invited through open registration on a "first-come-first-served" basis.

-   This tournament is open to teams from across the globe.

-   The registration fee per team:3000 RMB

     -    Re-fund policy: If your team needs to withdraw from the tournament before March 8(Beijing Time) we will try to replace your team with teams on the waiting list. If we find a replacement we will re-fund the full amount. If teams withdraw or fail to lock in lineup after March 8(Beijing Time) no refund will be made.

-   A total of 16 teams will be accepted through open registration. Registration is only valid once the payment has been completed. Limited slots available!

-   Initial seeding will be determined according to the VRS list as of March 2026.

     -    4.Pay for the registration fee within 24h to lock in/secure your spot


## Closed Qualifier

-   This stage will be held online.

-   The game server will be an online server located in China.

-   Format: Two double-elimination format Groups.

    -    Each group has eight teams

    -    All matches are Bo3.

    -    The top 2 teams from each group advance to the Playoffs

## Playoffs

-   This stage will be held offline.

-   Format: Single-elimination bracket.

    -    The final is Bo5, and all other matches are Bo3   

## Event Location：Shanghai, China
